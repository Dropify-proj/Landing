The Problem

Community engagement is broken.

Low Impact Airdrops

Traditional airdrops are often impersonal, unengaging, and fail to create lasting loyalty.

Complex Systems

Building custom, gamified reward systems is technically complex, costly, and time-consuming for projects.

Lack of Utility

Users receive tokens or NFTs with little immediate use, leading to quick sales and churn.

The Solution: Dropify

An all-in-one platform for meaningful digital rewards.

Gamified Minting

Allow users to mint unique digital collectibles, turning passive drops into active participation.

Burn-for-Rewards

Users can burn collectibles to claim tangible rewards, creating a fun, deflationary economic loop.

No-Code Platform

Projects can launch sophisticated reward campaigns in minutes, no developers required.

Business Model

Tiered SaaS Subscriptions

Projects subscribe to Dropify on a monthly or annual basis. Tiers are based on community size, number of campaigns, and feature access.

Platform Fee

A small percentage fee (e.g., 1-2%) is taken on the value of rewards distributed through the platform's burn mechanism.
